How to Log In to BlockFi

  • Visit the Official Site: Navigate to blockfi.com and click on the "Login" button at the top-right corner.
  • Enter Your Credentials: Provide your registered email address and secure password.
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): For added security, enter the verification code sent to your registered device.
  • Access Your Account: Once authenticated, explore your dashboard for trading, borrowing, and account management.
